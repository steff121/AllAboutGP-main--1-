Why You Should Adopt a Guinea Pig

  • When you adopt you're creating space in a rescue centre so you're effectively helping more animals than just the ones that you adopt.
  • If you adopt a pair of guinea pigs then it's likely they will already have an established bond.
  • Rescues will ensure that prospective pet owners are well aware to ensure that they are the right fit to meet the guinea pig’s care needs.
  • Guinea pigs are great pets for children and live long lives compared to hamsters.

Things to Consider Before Adopting

  • Guinea pigs do best with a companion.
  • Guinea pigs require a substantial commitment.
  • Guinea pigs require vegetables and hay, in addition to guinea pig food.
  • Guinea pigs require daily vitamin C supplementations.
  • Guinea pigs require a large enclosures.
  • Guinea pigs can be noisy.

How to Adopt

  • Step 1: Log into pet finder. https://www.petfinder.com/small-furry/cara-59900654/fl/tampa/humane-society-of-tampa-bay-fl316/
  • Step 2: Locate the find pet button and click on the small and fury subsection.
  • Step 3: Enter in your zip code.
  • Step 4: Locate the guinea pig of your choice.
  • Step 5: Double check any information about your soon to be pet and contact the adoption center!
