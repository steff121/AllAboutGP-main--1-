Fun Facts About Guinea Pigs!

  • They are not related to pigs at all.
  • They scent mark their stuff.
  • They only sleep for short periods.
  • They have an odd number of toes.

What Do Guinea Pigs Eat?

Some green vegetables that guinea pigs love to endulge in include:

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Green Leaf Lettuce
  • Kale
  • Cilantro
  • Parsley

Some fruits that guinea pigs love to snack on include:

  1. Kiwi
  2. Strawberry
  3. Pineapple
  4. Blueberry
  5. Watermelon
  6. Apple

What Kinds of Breeds of Guinea Pig Are There?

There are many different breeds of these little creatures such as:

  • Abyssinian Guinea Pig
  • Alpaca Guinea Pig
  • American Guinea Pig
  • Himalayan Guinea Pig
  • Peruvian Guinea Pig

Where Do Guinea Pigs Come From and Live?

Guinea pigs originated from the Andes Mountains of South America. They still live in the wild across South America today. The first members of the Caviidae family likely evolved sometime between 26 and 7 million years ago.
